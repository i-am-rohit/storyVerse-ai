StoryVerse AI Guide

Everything you need to create stories, audiobooks, and book summaries.

Getting Started

  1. Register or Login from the home screen.
  2. Open Stories to see your library or create a new tale.
  3. Use Audiobooks to turn any saved story into narrated audio.
  4. Use Books to upload PDF/DOCX/TXT and generate summaries.

Stories

Create a story

Go to Stories → New Story. Pick language, genre, age group, and story size, then describe your idea in the prompt box.

Continue & save

After generation, use Continue Story to extend the tale, then Save to store it in your library.

Stories library

Search, filter by source/audio, view full text, edit, open in Audiobooks, or delete from the Stories hub.

Sources

  • Generated — AI-written from your prompt
  • Uploaded — created from a book summary
  • Manual — saved without a prompt

Story Size

Choose how long your generated story should be in Story Settings → Story Size:

SizeApprox. wordsBest for
Short~150Quick bedtime tales
Medium~350Standard stories (default)
Long~600Richer plots
Extra Long~1,000Chapter-style narratives

Word counts are approximate. Continue Story adds more content based on the size you selected.

Audiobooks

Generate audio

Select a saved story, pick a voice (Western or Indian), choose background music, and click Generate Audio.

Voices

ElevenLabs powers premium voices when configured. Jennie works on the free tier. Demo audio is used if credits run out.

Your library

Track progress, play, regenerate with new voice/music, download, or delete audiobooks from the studio library.

Free Background Music

All background music in StoryVerse AI is 100% free. Music is composed locally on the server using our built-in Music Agent — no extra API keys, credits, or subscriptions required.

  • Indian Classical, Bollywood, Sitar, Bansuri, Tabla
  • Auto picks music matched to story genre & language
  • Mixed with narration in your browser during playback
  • Choose No Music for voice-only narration

Books & Summaries

Upload PDF, DOCX, or TXT files (up to 10 MB). For each book you can generate:

  • Short & detailed summaries
  • Chapter summaries & chapter shorts
  • Reading guide & best way to read
  • Narrate summary as audio

Account

  • Login / Register — username & password, or social (Gmail, Outlook, Facebook when configured)
  • Manage Users — separate superuser page for administrators
  • Profile — view your account from the user menu
  • Sessions stay signed in until you log out
